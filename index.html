The Double Bottom Line

The Problem

Our environment faces excessive plastic waste generated by standard cleaning products. Simultaneously, there is a severe lack of accessible employment opportunities for the PWD community.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning solutions via a circular "refill" economy, reducing single-use plastic waste. Simultaneously, we create a dignified, safe, and supportive working environment for Persons with Disabilities (PWDs).

The Solution

Re: Washify operates as a refill station and retail brand, selling premium liquid laundry and dishwashing detergents in reusable containers.

Our Vision

To transform the household cleaning industry through sustainability and inclusivity, establishing a brand where every purchase directly supports the employment of a PWD.

Inclusive Employment Impact

Re: Washify is intentionally designed as a safe, accessible workspace for a neurodiverse and differently-abled team.

Our facility features ramps, wide aisles, ergonomic stations, and sensory-friendly zones so Persons with Disabilities can work with confidence and comfort.

Through visual guides, color-coded workflows, and dedicated job coaching, we create long-term, dignified employment where our team can grow skills and income, not just complete tasks.
